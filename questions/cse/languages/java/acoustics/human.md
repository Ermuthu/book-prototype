answer:
  - "20 kHz"
explanation: "The normal audible range for a human ear is between 20 Hz and 20,000 Hz (which is 20 kHz). Frequencies below 20 Hz are called infrasonic, and frequencies above 20 kHz are called ultrasonic. Among the options, 20 kHz represents the upper limit of human hearing, whereas 50 kHz, 15,000 kHz, and 10,000 kHz are well into the ultrasonic range and cannot be heard by humans."
