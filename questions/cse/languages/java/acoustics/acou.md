answer:
  - "25 m"
explanation: "To hear a distinct echo, the time interval between the original sound and the reflected sound must be at least 0.1 seconds (persistence of hearing). The total distance traveled by the sound to the obstacle and back is $2d$. Using the formula $2d = v \times t$, we get $2d = 500 \times 0.1 = 50 \text{ m}$. Therefore, the minimum distance ($d$) is $50 / 2 = 25 \text{ m}$."
