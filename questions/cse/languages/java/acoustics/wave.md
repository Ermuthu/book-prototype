---
choices:
  - "speed"
  - "frequency"
  - "wavelength"
  - "amplitude"
answer:
  - "amplitude"
explanation: "When a sound wave is reflected into the same medium, its speed, frequency, and wavelength remains constant because the properties of the medium have not changed. However, some energy is usually absorbed by the obstacle or scattered, which causes a decrease in the amplitude (and consequently the intensity/loudness) of the reflected wave."
