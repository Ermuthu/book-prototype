answer:
  - "ohm"
explanation: "The SI unit of electrical resistance is the ohm, symbolized by the Greek letter omega ($\Omega$). It is defined as the resistance between two points of a conductor when a constant potential difference of 1 volt applied to these points produces a current of 1 ampere. 'Mho' is the unit for conductance, 'joule' for energy, and 'ohm meter' for resistivity."
