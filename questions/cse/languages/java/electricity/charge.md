choices:
  - "Rate of change of charge is electrical power."
  - "Rate of change of charge is current."
  - "Rate of change of energy is current."
  - "Rate of change of current is charge."
answer:
  - "Rate of change of charge is current."
explanation: "Electric current ($I$) is defined as the rate at which electric charge ($Q$) flows past a point in a circuit over a period of time ($t$). The mathematical relationship is expressed as $I = \frac{dQ}{dt}$ or $I = \frac{Q}{t}$. Power is the rate of change of energy, not charge."
