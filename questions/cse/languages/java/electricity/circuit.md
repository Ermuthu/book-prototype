choices:
  - "The switch produces electricity."
  - "Closing the switch completes the circuit."
  - "Closing the switch breaks the circuit."
  - "The bulb is getting charged."
answer:
  - "Closing the switch completes the circuit."
explanation: "In an electric circuit, current can only flow if there is a continuous, unbroken path from the positive terminal to the negative terminal of the power source. Closing the switch acts as a bridge, completing the loop and allowing electrons to flow through the filament of the bulb, causing it to glow."
