answer:
  - "electrical energy"
explanation: "Kilowatt-hour (kWh) is a unit of energy. Since Power is the rate of doing work ($P = E/t$), Energy can be calculated as Power multiplied by Time ($E = P \times t$). A kilowatt is a unit of power, and an hour is a unit of time; therefore, their product represents the total electrical energy consumed. This is the standard unit used by electricity boards to bill consumers."
